Our Package

Pulo Cinta

Escape to Pulo Cinta, Gorontalo, Indonesia, a hidden paradise where you can unwind in a boutique retreat. Experience the enchanting beauty of Celebes’ pristine island, offering a mystical journey into serene horizons and vibrant underwater wonders.






Saronde Island

Saronde Islands, just 40 minutes from Gorontalo Airport, is a stunning small island known for its white sandy beaches, clear blue waters, and serene pine tree surroundings. With over 16,000 annual visitors, including cruise ships and yachts, it’s a must-visit paradise in Indonesia!






Bahia Tomini

Bahia Tomini in Central Sulawesi, Indonesia, is a serene paradise where nature thrives, offering a peaceful escape from the chaos of modern life.









Tanah Lot

Visit Tanah Lot, Bali's iconic Hindu temple perched on a rocky sea outcrop, and immerse yourself in its spiritual beauty. Explore the lush Jatiluwih Green Land, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, before ending your day at the stunning Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, one of Bali's most photographed gems.

Jimbaran Beach
